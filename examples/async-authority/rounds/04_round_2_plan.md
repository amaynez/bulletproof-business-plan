# Hardened Business Plan v2: The Async Authority (Managed)

## Mission
To build a high-leverage educational ecosystem that runs on "Strategic Presence," allowing the founder to remain off-camera for discovery while delivering high-value coaching through productized systems.

## Strategy (Round 2 Hardening)
1. **The Discovery Engine (Delegated)**: Outsourced "Faceless Reels" production using a dedicated content agency. This removes the 2-3 hour/day content creation burden.
2. **The "Hybrid Presence" Model**: 
    - **Top of Funnel**: 100% Faceless.
    - **Middle of Funnel (Email/Foundation)**: High-quality screen-shares/Loom-style presentations (Value > Face).
    - **Bottom of Funnel (Mastermind)**: Weekly live sessions. Founder presence is used as a "High-Value Asset," not a "Content Mill."
3. **Frictionless Lead Capture**: 
    - Use ManyChat's **Email-in-DM** capture (one-tap) to minimize the "Email Wall" drop-off.
    - Automated welcome sequence with a "48-Hour Fast-Action" discount to maintain front-end liquidity.
4. **Scale Infrastructure (The Staffing Plan)**:
    - **Month 1-3**: Solo + Content Agency.
    - **Month 4+**: Hire a "Success Coach" (part-time) from the top-performing community members to handle 80% of moderation and Tier 1 support.

## Revenue Model (Stabilized)
- Adjusted Front-end: $7,500/mo (reflects 25% drop-off from email capture).
- Backend Upsells: $24,850/mo.
- Continuity: $9,700/mo.
- **Total Revenue**: ~$42,050/mo.
- **Costs**: $6,500/mo (Content Agency $3k, Mastermind Coach $2k, Tools $1.5k).
- **Net Profit**: ~$35,550/mo (Lower margin, but 10x higher defensibility and lower founder labor).
