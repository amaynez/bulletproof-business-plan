# Hardened Business Plan v1: The Async Authority

## Mission
To build a defensible, multi-channel educational brand for remote professionals, using Instagram as a discovery wedge and email/community as the revenue engine.

## Strategy (The "Hardened" Pivot)
1. **Discovery Wedge**: Post 2-3 high-authority Reels (using proprietary "Aesthetic Logic" templates) to drive traffic.
2. **De-risking (Lead Capture)**: The "Comment [ACCESS]" trigger now sends a link to a **High-Value Lead Magnet** (e.g., "The Async Salary Spreadsheet"). This requires an email address, moving the relationship off-platform immediately.
3. **The Offer Stack (LTV Expansion)**:
    - **Tier 1 (Front-end)**: $97 "Async Foundations" Course (Automated Email Upsell).
    - **Tier 2 (Core)**: $497 "Authority Mastermind" (6-week intensive).
    - **Tier 3 (Continuity)**: $97/mo "Async Guild" Private Community (Networking + Weekly coaching).
4. **Moat (Proprietary IP)**: Implementation of "The Async-Authority Framework"—a specific, documentable methodology for career advancement that cannot be easily cloned.

## Marketing
- 70% Organic Discovery (Instagram/X).
- 30% Retargeting Ads (Meta/Google) to email list leads (diversifies traffic sources).

## Revenue Model (Projected)
- Front-end Sales: $9,700/mo.
- Mastermind Upsells (5% conv): $24,850/mo.
- Continuity (100 members): $9,700/mo.
- **Total Revenue**: ~$44,250/mo.
- **Costs**: $2,500/mo (Ads, Skool, Email CRM, Virtual Assistant).
- **Net Profit**: ~$41,750/mo.
