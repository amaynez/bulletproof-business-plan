# Hardened Business Plan v3 (Final): The Async Standard

## Mission
To become the industry standard for asynchronous work methodologies, delivering certified training to remote professionals and B2B corporate teams via a multi-channel discovery engine.

## Strategy (Final Hardening)
1. **The Asset (The Async Standard™)**: Transition from a "Course" to a **Certification Body**. The value is now the "Async Standard Certificate," which has career-advancement value. This separates the asset from the founder's persona.
2. **Multi-Channel Flywheel**:
    - **Short-Form (IG/TikTok)**: Viral discovery and "trailers."
    - **Long-Form (YouTube/SEO)**: Educational authority and searchability.
    - **Owned (Email/Substack)**: Relationship and conversion.
3. **B2B Pivot**: Introduction of the **"Async-Ready Teams" Package**. Corporate licenses for remote teams (10-50 seats). This provides high-ticket, resilient revenue that is independent of individual discretionary spending.
4. **Governance Layer**: The community is now moderated by "Lead Practitioners" (certified alumni), creating a self-sustaining ecosystem that functions without founder intervention.

## Revenue Model (Enterprise Scale)
- B2C Individual Certifications: $15,000/mo.
- B2B Corporate Licenses: $35,000/mo.
- Continuity (Pro-Community): $12,000/mo.
- **Total Revenue**: ~$62,000/mo.
- **Costs**: $15,000/mo (B2B Sales Rep $5k, Content Team $6k, Tech/Ops $4k).
- **Net Profit**: ~$47,000/mo.
- **Acquisition Multiplier**: 4x - 6x EBITDA (due to B2B contracts and non-founder dependency).
