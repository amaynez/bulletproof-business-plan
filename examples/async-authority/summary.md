# Evolution: The Async Standard (Case Study)

This case study demonstrates how the `bullet-proof-business-plan` skill transforms a fragile "growth hack" into a defensible, investable business asset.

## Round 0: The Hypothesis
A faceless Instagram channel selling a $97 digital course via ManyChat "Comment-to-DM" automation, relying 100% on organic Reels reach.
- **Artifact**: [00_original_plan.md](00_original_plan.md)

---

## The Hardening Journey

### Round 1: Platform Independence
- **Objection**: 90% Probability of Rejection due to total reliance on Meta's algorithm.
- **Pivot**: Shifted from Direct Checkout to **Email Lead Capture**. ManyChat now builds a platform-independent email list first.
- **Artifacts**: [Adversarial Review](rounds/01_round_1_review.md) | [Hardened Plan v1](rounds/02_round_1_plan.md)

### Round 2: Operational Scalability
- **Objection**: 85% Probability of Rejection due to founder burnout (managing 3 tiers + content + coaching).
- **Pivot**: Implemented a **Delegation Layer** (Content Agency + Community Success Coach) and productized the coaching curriculum.
- **Artifacts**: [Adversarial Review](rounds/03_round_2_review.md) | [Hardened Plan v2](rounds/04_round_2_plan.md)

### Round 3: The Investable Asset
- **Objection**: 85% Probability of Rejection because the brand was tied to the founder's personal authority (Exit Blocker).
- **Pivot**: Transitioned to **The Async Standard™ Certification**. The asset is now a methodology that can be sold/licensed without the founder's presence.
- **Artifacts**: [Adversarial Review](rounds/05_round_3_review.md) | [Final Hardened Plan](rounds/06_final_plan.md)

## Final Status: Bulletproof
The business is now a multi-channel, B2B-ready certification body with high-margin recurring revenue and a clear exit path.
