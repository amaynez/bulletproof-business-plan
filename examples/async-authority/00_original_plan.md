# Business Plan: The Faceless Growth Engine

## Mission
To build a high-margin, automated revenue engine by selling an online course ("The Digital Nomad Blueprint") via Instagram Reels automation.

## Strategy
1. **Content**: Post 2-3 faceless Reels per day using stock footage and AI voiceovers.
2. **Growth**: Use the "Comment [FREEDOM] for the link" call-to-action on every post.
3. **Automation**: Use ManyChat to detect the comment and automatically DM the user a direct link to the checkout page.
4. **Product**: A $97 digital course hosted on Teachable.

## Marketing
- Focus on high-reach keywords: Passive Income, Financial Freedom, Remote Work.
- No paid ads; rely purely on organic viral reach of Reels.

## Revenue Model
- Target: 100 sales per month.
- Monthly Revenue: $9,700.
- Costs: $0 (stock footage is free, AI voice is free-tier, ManyChat is $15/mo).
- Net Profit: ~$9,685/mo.

## Operations
- 100% automated once content is scheduled.
- Solo-operator model.
